History

The Equalizer Basketball club was founded in the year 2024. The club was became into existance by the efforts of two young individuals, Agnes Urupua and Tiriman Masi. These two loved the game of basketball and wanted the young ones in their community to expose them to the sport and to give them the oppotunity to showcase their talents. Port Moresby Northeast do not have a basketball team and now it does. The club is currently planning an expansion, with the induction of a Junior squard. The future looks bright for this young club.
